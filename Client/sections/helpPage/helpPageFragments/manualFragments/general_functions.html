General Functions

General functions of the web application.

Explanation

First of all, the application provides two views of the current condition of carriers: Circles View and Bar Chart View


Circles View

Selecting the Circle View shows a list of carriers and the current state of their energy consumption. Circle View has two different sub-views that offer data based on what type of contamination the user expects to detect.

  • 1. Creeping Contamination – energy consumption for the carrier is calculated as the average energy consumption (current iteration) of the carrier to the average energy consumption of that carrier from the 1st 10% of the iterations
  • 2. Continuous Contamination – energy consumption for the carrier is calculated as the average energy consumption of the current carrier over average energy consumption of all the carriers for all iterations

Green circles depict no or minimal increase in energy consumption, yellow – slight increase and red – a significant increase.

In order to switch between the two views there is a toggle button under the circles.
